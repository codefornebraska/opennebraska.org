Open Nebraska

Open/Nebraska is a civic innovation organization serving the citizens of the great state of Nebraska. Founded in 2012, our mission is to solve community problems through civic application development, open data advocacy, and tech education. We aim to drive innovation and community engagement in Nebraska by making local information more accessible to the public.
